Privacy Policy

Introduction

We care about protecting the privacy and confidentiality of our clients' and visitors' data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in relation to our consulting services. By using our website and services, you consent to the practices described below.

What Information We Collect

1. Personal Data

While using our consulting services, we may collect your personal data such as name, surname, contact details, and other information you choose to provide. This data is collected when you fill out forms, sign up for services, or contact us via email or other communication channels.

2. Usage Data

We may collect information about how you use our website. This data can include your IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and date and time of visit. This information helps us better understand our users' needs and improve our materials.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance the user experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and analyze how you use our site. You can manage cookie settings in your browser, but disabling them may affect the functionality of the website.

How We Use Your Information

1. Providing Consulting Services

Your personal data is used to provide our consulting services, including responding to inquiries, delivering educational materials, and tailoring content to your needs.

2. Communication

We may use your contact information to communicate about service updates, additional materials, and other information related to our consulting services. You have the right to opt-out of such notifications at any time.

3. Analytics and Research

Usage data and cookies are used to analyze trends, manage the website, and improve educational content. This helps us provide materials better suited to the needs of our clients.

4. Legal Requirements

Your data may be used to comply with legal requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. In case of legal obligations, data may be shared with appropriate authorities.

Sharing Data

1. Third-Party Services

We may share your personal data with external service providers who assist us in running the website or delivering consulting services. These providers are obligated to protect your data and cannot use it for other purposes.

2. Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of part of the business, your personal data may be transferred to the new entity. You will be notified, and your rights will remain unaffected.

3. Legal Compliance

We may disclose your personal data when required by law or in response to legitimate requests by public authorities.

Data Security

We protect your personal data using appropriate technical and organizational measures. Despite our efforts, we cannot guarantee complete data security, so we recommend using strong passwords and exercising caution when sharing your data.

Your Rights

1. Access and Correction

You have the right to access your personal data and request corrections. If you notice that your data is incorrect or incomplete, please contact us for updates.

2. Deletion

You can request the deletion of your personal data from our systems, provided that there are no legal requirements for retaining it.

3. Restriction of Processing

You have the right to request the restriction of processing your data under certain circumstances, such as a dispute regarding the accuracy of the data.

4. Data Portability

You can request a copy of your personal data in a format that allows for its transfer to another service provider.

5. Objection

You can object to the processing of your personal data for marketing or other legitimate interests. We respect your decision and will adjust the processing accordingly.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page regularly for the latest information.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about the Privacy Policy or how we process data, please contact us. We are here to help.
